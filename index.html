About

I am a Mathematics PhD student at Duke University, working on quantum error correction under the supervision of Robert Calderbank. I received Duke's Certificate in College Teaching.

I completed my undergraduate studies at the University of Chicago with a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics (Honors) and was advised by Jack Cowan, Alex Eskin, and Peter May.

I am interested in quantum error correction, autonomous research, mathematical superintelligence, diffusion models, world models, and AI safety.
